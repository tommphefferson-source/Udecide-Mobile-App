# UDecide — Commercial Video Prompt for ChatGPT / Sora / Video AI

---

## PROMPT TO PASTE INTO CHATGPT

> **Instruction to ChatGPT:** Use this prompt to write a full 60-second commercial script, shot list, and voiceover for the UDecide mobile app. The reference screenshots are included separately.

---

### PROMPT:

You are a creative director at a top political advertising agency. Write a **60-second mobile app commercial** for **UDecide**, a free, nonpartisan political and voter information app for U.S. citizens.

**Brand identity:**
- Name: UDecide
- Tagline: "Your vote. Your voice. Your decision."
- Colors: Navy blue (#0D1B2A), crimson red (#C41E3A), white
- Logo: Bold "U" with a "VOTE" badge, "DECIDE" text beneath — patriotic but modern
- Tone: Empowering, nonpartisan, trustworthy, accessible to all Americans regardless of party

**The commercial should include all of the following sections — write a full script with timecodes, shot descriptions, on-screen text, and voiceover:**

---

**[0:00 – 0:08] — HOOK / PROBLEM**
Open on real, emotionally relatable scenes of everyday Americans feeling confused or frustrated by politics — too much noise, too many conflicting headlines, not knowing who represents them or when elections are. End with a simple question on screen: *"What if understanding politics was actually easy?"*

---

**[0:08 – 0:16] — APP REVEAL**
The UDecide logo animates in on a navy background (reference: Login Screen). The app name and tagline appear. Voiceover introduces UDecide as the free, nonpartisan app built to help every American be an informed voter.

---

**[0:16 – 0:28] — FEATURE SHOWCASE (rapid montage)**
Show the following app screens in sequence (2–3 seconds each), with on-screen captions and brief voiceover for each:

1. **Dashboard** (reference: Home Screen) — "Everything you need, in one place."
2. **Representatives** (reference: Reps Screen) — "Know exactly who represents you — federal, state, county, and city."
3. **Elections & Ballots** (reference: Elections Screen) — "Never miss an election. Registration deadlines, polling places, and real ballot data."
4. **Legislation Tracker** (reference: Legislation Screen) — "Track real bills in Congress and your state legislature."
5. **Political Parties** (reference: Parties Screen) — "Understand every party's platform — no spin, no bias."
6. **Voter Status Tools** (reference: Voter Tools Screen) — "Check your registration, find your polling place, and request your absentee ballot."
7. **Gemini Fact Checker** (reference: Fact Checker Screen) — "Ask our AI anything about politics — and get a straight, sourced answer."

---

**[0:28 – 0:40] — EMPOWERMENT MOMENT**
Slow down. Show a diverse group of Americans — young, old, different backgrounds, different viewpoints — all looking at their phones with confidence. On-screen text: *"Red. Blue. Or somewhere in between. UDecide is for all of us."* Voiceover emphasizes that UDecide does not endorse any candidate, party, or policy — it just gives you the facts so YOU can decide.

---

**[0:40 – 0:52] — SOCIAL PROOF / CREDIBILITY**
Show animated stat cards:
- "Live data from Google Civic Information API"
- "Real-time federal bills from Congress.gov"
- "AI fact-checking powered by Google Gemini"
- "100% nonpartisan — no endorsements, ever"

Voiceover: "Powered by real government data. Fact-checked by AI. Designed to inform — never to influence."

---

**[0:52 – 1:00] — CALL TO ACTION**
End card on navy background. UDecide logo pulses in. Tagline appears: *"Your vote. Your voice. Your decision."*
Download badge animations: App Store and Google Play.
Voiceover: "Download UDecide — free on iOS and Android. Because an informed vote is the most powerful vote of all."

---

**Additional creative direction:**
- Music: Uplifting but grounded orchestral/indie hybrid — think Aaron Copland meets a modern indie film score. No partisan anthem energy.
- Pacing: Starts calm and relatable, builds momentum through the feature montage, lands on an emotionally resonant and dignified close.
- Casting: Show real-looking Americans of diverse ages, ethnicities, and geographies. Avoid any visual cues of party affiliation (no red hats, no party signs).
- On-screen phone mockups: Use iPhone/Android device frames showing the actual app screenshots provided.
- No celebrity endorsements. No political figures. No party symbols.

**Deliverables from ChatGPT:**
1. Full 60-second script with timecodes
2. Shot-by-shot description for a video director
3. Full voiceover transcript
4. Suggested music cue descriptions per scene
5. On-screen text/title card copy for each segment
6. Optional: 15-second cut-down version for social media ads

## NOTES FOR VIDEO PRODUCTION TOOLS (Sora, RunwayML, Pika, etc.)

If generating video clips with an AI video tool, use these prompts per scene:

- **Hook:** Cinematic close-up of hands scrolling through overwhelming news headlines on a phone. Warm, slightly desaturated color grade. 4K. Shallow depth of field.
- **App reveal:** Dark navy background. White and red logo animates in from center with a subtle pulse glow. Clean product reveal style. No camera movement.
- **Feature montage:** iPhone 15 Pro in hand, rotating slowly, screen reflecting real app UI. Quick cuts, each 2.5 seconds. Bright, clean studio lighting on white.
- **Empowerment moment:** Diverse group of Americans in everyday settings — a kitchen, a park bench, a college campus — looking at their phones with expressions of understanding and confidence. Golden hour lighting. Soft focus background.
- **End card:** Navy background. Logo center frame. Subtle animated star/flag particles. Clean white text. App store badges slide in from bottom.
